Aerial

This was inspired by "Turtles, Termites, and Traffic Jams".

Imagine a cartesian grid occupied by many, many turtles, each moving according to the same set of local rules.

In this simulation, each turtle also carries a musical instrument and plays a note from the C major scale.  Depending on what it hears, it tries to play a note that harmonizes with its sonic environment.  This in turn, affects the notes other turtles hear.

...Or don't imagine that...I mean...moving colors and stuff.
HTML5 isn't supported : (

Controls

spacebar: pause

'c': randomly change the color scheme

'i': toggle-view hypothetical turtles

'r': randomly reposition hypothetical turtles

'1','2', or '3': presets

  • Preset 1:
    20 turtles on a 25 x 25 grid playing xylophones

  • Preset 2:
    10 turtles on a 100 x 100 grid ringing handbells

  • Preset 3:
    15 turtles on a 50 x 50 grid plucking harps with broken 'A' strings
